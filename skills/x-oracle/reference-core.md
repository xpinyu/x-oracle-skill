# X-Oracle Core Reference

This file defines the BaZi evidence model. Default user-facing output should be technical enough to be convincing: name the Four Pillars evidence, explain the classical principle, then translate it into concrete life implications.

## Evidence Hierarchy
Use this order when reasoning:

1. Four pillars: year, month, day, hour.
2. Day master: the day heavenly stem is the self-reference point.
3. Month branch and season: primary strength and climate signal.
4. Stems, branches, hidden stems: visible and latent forces.
5. Ten gods: relational functions around the day master.
6. Five elements: generation, control, excess, scarcity, and flow.
7. Branch/stem interactions: combinations, clashes, harms, punishments, transformations.
8. Luck cycles and annual pillars: timing windows, not deterministic events.

Do not give a major conclusion if it cannot be tied to at least one item above.

## Evidence-First Writing Style
Default reports must not hide BaZi terms. Use this pattern:

```markdown
八字依据：[pillar / day master / month command / ten god / hidden stem / interaction]
古法原则：[the classical rule or reasoning school, not a fake verbatim quote]
直断：[clear judgment]
现实落点：[how it appears in personality, career, relationship, wealth, or timing]
```

Use Chinese technical terms directly: 日主、月令、格局、得令、得地、透干、通根、藏干、十神、财官印食、伤官、七杀、比劫、合冲刑害、用神、忌神、大运、流年.

Do not over-explain beginner definitions unless the user is new to the topic. A convincing report should feel like a practitioner reading from the chart, not like a generic personality essay.

## Classical Principle Map
Use classical sources as reasoning anchors. Do not fabricate exact quotes unless you can verify them.

- 《子平真诠》: prioritize month command, structure, clear/pure vs mixed patterns, and whether a formation is usable.
- 《滴天髓》: emphasize qi flow, balance, strength/weakness, source and outlet, disease and medicine logic.
- 《穷通宝鉴》: emphasize climate adjustment, especially cold/heat/dry/wet and seasonal suitability.
- 《渊海子平》: use foundational ten-god, six-relationship, and pillar interpretation rules.
- 《三命通会》: use broad synthesis, special patterns, shen-sha only as supporting evidence.

Classical books may disagree by school. If the chart is ambiguous, state the competing readings and why one is stronger.

## Day Master and Season
Start with the day master, then judge whether the season supports or drains it. This must be explicit in the report.

- Supported day master: focus on expression, responsibility, wealth conversion, and controlled expansion.
- Weak day master: focus on support, learning, allies, recovery, and avoiding overextension.
- Overheated, overcold, overdry, or overwet chart: climate correction may matter more than simple strength count.

Avoid simplistic element counting. Month command, rootedness, hidden stems, and transformations matter more than raw totals. If element counts are included, explain why they are secondary.

## Ten-God Reading
Name the ten gods directly, then explain their life functions:

- 比肩 / 劫财: self-force, peers, competition, autonomy, boundary pressure.
- 食神 / 伤官: expression, craft, output, rebellion, taste, creativity.
- 正财 / 偏财: resources, money handling, opportunity, transactional skill.
- 正官 / 七杀: responsibility, power, rules, pressure, ambition, threat response.
- 正印 / 偏印: learning, protection, memory, intuition, abstraction, support.

Look for structure:

- Dominant output with weak control: talent and expression need discipline.
- Strong control with insufficient support: pressure may become anxiety or overcompliance.
- Strong wealth with weak self: opportunity can outpace capacity.
- Strong resource with little output: learning may not convert into visible results.
- Peer-heavy charts: autonomy and competition become major themes.

## Five-Element Flow
Read element flow as a system:

- Wood: growth, planning, ethics, expansion.
- Fire: visibility, expression, recognition, warmth.
- Earth: stabilization, trust, accumulation, mediation.
- Metal: structure, rules, precision, judgment.
- Water: cognition, mobility, emotion, strategy.

Prefer direct chart-based statements:

- "日主得令但财星透出，能担财则财为用，不能担财则财成压力。"
- "杀重有印，先看能否成杀印相生；若印弱无根，则压力大于权柄。"
- "食伤旺而官星受制，适合靠技术表达、内容产品、方案输出破局，但忌无规则地冲撞权威。"

Avoid fatalistic statements.

## Relationship Reading
Use relationship claims carefully. Evidence may include day branch, spouse star, combinations/clashes involving the day branch, and current timing.

Write in terms of chart evidence:

- 日支状态
- 配偶星状态
- 日支与月时年支的合冲刑害
- 大运流年是否引动夫妻宫或配偶星
- 财官印食是否形成稳定流通

Do not predict marriage, divorce, cheating, infertility, or death as certainty.

## Career and Wealth
Career analysis should connect:

- Officer/killing: structure, leadership, responsibility, external pressure.
- Output: creativity, product, communication, public expression.
- Wealth: market, monetization, clients, assets, management of resources.
- Resource: study, credentials, protection, research, institutional support.
- Peer: collaboration, competition, entrepreneurship, cofounder dynamics.

Wealth analysis must distinguish:

- Earning ability
- Holding ability
- Risk appetite
- Timing window
- Whether opportunity matches capacity

Never give financial instructions as investment advice.

## Luck Cycles and Annual Timing
Use timing as phase themes, with explicit activation logic:

- A luck cycle changes the environment, not the whole person.
- A year activates or pressures existing chart structures.
- Strong signals repeat when natal chart, luck cycle, and annual pillar point in the same direction.
- If signals conflict, explain the conflict instead of forcing one conclusion.

Use phrases like "此运引动", "流年触发", "应事更可能落在", "高概率主题是", and "需要看现实选择承接". Avoid "will definitely."

## Confidence Notes
Lower confidence when:

- Birth time is unknown or approximate.
- Birth time is within 30 minutes of a time-branch boundary.
- Birthplace/longitude is missing and solar-time correction is not possible.
- Date is near a solar-term boundary.
- Calendar type is unclear.
- Gender is missing for luck-cycle direction.

State what changes if the uncertain input changes.
